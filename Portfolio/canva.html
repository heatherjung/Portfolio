Canva has a library of templates that you could choose from that enables you to create designs quickly and easily. My design approach are usually elegant and minimalistic styles. The usual routine that I take looks like:

  1. choosing a background template
  2. adding the text
  3. adjusting the colors
  4. inserting icons as necessary

By the way, I love using high quality images from unsplash.com for my backgrounds.
